Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 11, 2020

We respect the privacy of visitors to The Vitamin H website (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy explains how The Vitamin H (“we” or “us”) collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects the information that we collect from you when you visit our Site, as well as your rights and choices regarding the information that we collect. This Privacy Policy does not apply to information collected from other sources, whether offline or online. This Privacy Policy also does not apply to any third-party websites to which the Site may link or from which the Site may be linked. We do not control the content or privacy practices of third-party websites.

Privacy of Personal Information

For purposes of this Privacy Policy, “personal information” means any information that may be used to identify an individual. Personal information includes information such as your name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, and other similar information.

Collection of Personal Information

To access updated features of the Site, you must first complete a brief registration form. During registration, you are required to provide certain personal information (such as name and email). By registering, you agree to receive certain communications from us. After registration, you may be given the option as to whether or not you want to receive other types of communication from us.

We collect personal information about you, including personal information that you provide when you register to use the Site. You may access and browse portions of the Site without disclosing personal information. However, providing certain requested information may be necessary for you to participate in particular features or services described on the Site.

Use of Personal Information

Personal information collected through our Site is used by us to, among other things, process and manage registrations, operate and improve our Site, track the Site’s usage policies and statistics, and for other purposes pursuant to your consent. We also use your personal information to communicate with you, such as confirming your registration or informing you of significant changes to this Privacy Policy.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We may provide personal information to other companies who provide services on our behalf, such as creating your account, handling communications, hosting the Site, and delivery of content and services.

We may access, use, or disclose your personal information for reasons including compliance with the law, protection of our rights, safety of users, enforcement of our Terms of Use, or in connection with corporate transactions or with your consent.

Access to Personal Information

To update or change the personal information you have provided to us, or to unsubscribe, please email contactus@thevitaminh.org.

Security

We have implemented procedures to help protect the personal information that you provide to us. However, no method of transmitting or storing electronic data is ever completely secure, and we cannot guarantee that such information will never be accessed, used, or released in a manner inconsistent with this Privacy Policy.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Should we learn that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete that information from our database.

Our Practices with Respect to Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information about your visit, including pages viewed, links clicked, and other actions taken on the Site. We may use cookies and web beacons to gather non-personal information, which may be combined with personal information for internal purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. We will notify you about material changes by sending a notice to the e-mail address you provided or by placing a prominent notice on the Site.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contactus@thevitaminh.org.
